My Task Templates Overview

Task templates include default tasks to be performed for a construction project, all of which are fully customizable to fit your needs.

If you have multiple templates, you will be prompted to choose your task template when scheduling your lots. With task templates,  you will be able to schedule your different projects independent of each other and still manage each lot or project from your running schedules.

From the My Task Templates screen, you can create new templates from scratch, create new templates by copying existing templates, edit the tasks of existing templates, and share templates with other users.
