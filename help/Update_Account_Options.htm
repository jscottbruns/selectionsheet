Update Account Options

To update your SelectionSheet.Com account information and preferences, do the following:

  1. Click Account Options in the top left-hand corner of the SelectionSheet.Com screen. The Account Preferences screen displays with the following tabbed screens:

    • General

      Use the General Account Information screen to update contact information and change your password.

    • Schedule

      Use the Schedule Preferences screen to update various default colors, styles, and views.

      Below are the sections of the Schedule Preferences Screen:

      • Task Status and Colors
        Depending on the status of a task (Confirmed, In-Progress, etc...), the task will be different in color, style, etc. Make any necessary changes. Click RESTORE DEFAULTS to restore the values to their original settings.

      • Midnight Function
        If the Midnight Robot is ON, every midnight the SelectionSheet.Com robot filters through your lots. For each task found on this date with a status of In-Progress or Confirmed, a day is added to its duration. For each task found with a status of Non-Confirmed or Hold are moved to the next day (tomorrow). If the Email Notification checkbox is checked, a report is sent to your email address to notify you when any tasks are moved.

      • Printable Schedule
        Use these options (Show Reminders and Show Appointments) to change what displays with your schedule when you print it.

      • Running Schedule Preferences
        Use the Default View drop down list to specify the default view (1 Week, 2 Weeks, 1 Month) for your running schedules.

    • Home Page

      Use the Home Page Preferences to configure and change the layout of your SelectionSheet.Com Home Page.

  3. Make any necessary changes.

  4. Click Update to save.
    Note:You must click Updateto save changes to...

    ...an account preference screen before selecting another preference screen.
