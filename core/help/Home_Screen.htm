Home Screen

The SelectionSheet.Com Home screen allows quick access to many functions.

Items listed on the Home screen include:

  • Running Schedule
    If you have already added lots and they are currently in production, your running schedule displays on this screen

  • Message Center
    Displays the number of new emails and appointments not related to lots. You can access your emails and appointments from this area.

  • Discussion Forum
    Lists recent posts from the SelectionSheet.Com forum

  • Your Calendar
    Provides quick access to important dates and appointment detail

  • Weather Center
    Provides at-a-glance weather conditions and forecast.

  • My Contacts
    Easily search and find contacts

  • Resources
    Provides links to further information regarding SelectionSheet.Com
