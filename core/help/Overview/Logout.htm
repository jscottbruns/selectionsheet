Logout

To log out of SelectionSheet.Com, do the following:

Click Logout in the top left-hand corner of the SelectionSheet.Com screen.
Note: To Log in again, keep the SelectionSheet.Com screen open...

...and do the following:

  1. Enter your user name in the UserName field.

  2. Enter your password in the Password field.

  3. Click Go. The SelectionSheet.Com Home screen displays.
