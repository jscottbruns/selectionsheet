View/Update Running Schedules

After you schedule a lot for construction, the Running Schedules screen provides you with a real time view of all activities. Access specific attributes (tasks, appointments, reminders) with a single mouse click.

Note:If you created subcontractors and...

...specified the two different subcontractor for a single task, a conflict prompt will display. Simply follow the instructions and select the appropriate subcontractor for the task, and then click Schedule.

To view and update running schedules, do the following:

  1. From the My Active & Pending Lots screen, click View Schedule under the appropriate lot, or select Schedules > Running Schedules.

  2. (Optional) You can do the following while viewing the Running Schedules screen:

    • You can scroll through the schedules by clicking the left/right arrows to either side of the month name.
      Note: The inside arrows, allow you to...

      ...move forward/backward 1 week at a time, while the outside arrows allow you to navigate forward/backward 1 month at a time.

    • You can change the way you view the schedule by selecting the appropriate view from the drop down list.

    • Click a task in the appropriate schedule to view more information about that task.
      Note: When you click on a task...

      ...the Edit a Task screen displays. Use this screen to move the task to a different day, change the tasks status or duration, add a comment, or specify a subcontractor for the task. Click UPDATE to save.
